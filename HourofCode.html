It is a great pleasure to inform you that as Team 7, we celebrated computer science week, which runs from December 4-10, by holding our #Hour of Code event at Baku Secondary School No. 249 on November 2. During the event, first we provided information about the possible opportunities at ADA University, and then we taught the fifth grade, which consists of 20 students, the basic concepts of programming by using code.org. As ADA students, our primary goal was to motivate younger generations and increase their curiosity about programming. We would like to express our gratitude to the school organizer for giving us this chance and to the informatics teacher for her support.
